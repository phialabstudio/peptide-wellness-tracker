  GLP-1 & PEPTIDE WELLNESS TRACKER
  Build: June 2026  |  Single-file web app (offline, private)
===============================================================================

A calm, all-in-one tracker for people on GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy,
Mounjaro, Zepbound, Saxenda). Log injections, side effects, weight, protein,
and hydration in one place — then generate a clean, printable summary to bring
to your doctor.

The app runs entirely in your browser. No account, no internet connection, no
data ever leaves your device.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  HOW TO USE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Open the index.html file in any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge,
   Firefox). Double-click it, or drag it into a browser window.

2. Go to Settings -> Your Profile and enter your name, age, and height. These
   power the dashboard greeting, BMI, and the heart-rate calculator.

3. Set your units under Settings -> Units & Display (lbs/kg, inches/cm,
   oz/ml/cups). The whole app adapts automatically.

4. Start logging. Everything saves automatically as you go.

TIP: Bookmark the page or add it to your phone's home screen for one-tap access.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  FEATURES BY TAB
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DASHBOARD
  - Personalized greeting + daily motivational quote
  - "How are you feeling today?" quick mood check-in
  - Today's Focus (rotating gentle reminders)
  - Next Injection / Last Injection cards (shows total injections logged)
  - Side Effects Today (one-tap symptom logging)
  - Gentle Fuel Reminder (protein + water progress bars)
  - Weight Progress card with color-coded BMI
  - One-tap access to your printable Doctor Summary

INJECTION LOG
  - Running count of total injections logged
  - Log date, time, medication, dose, injection site, protocol phase, notes
  - Default medications: Semaglutide (Ozempic / Wegovy),
    Tirzepatide (Mounjaro / Zepbound), Liraglutide (Saxenda)
  - Add your own custom medications anytime
  - Injection sites include Abdomen Left/Right and Left/Right Thigh
    (to help you rotate)
  - Filter history by medication or phase; edit or delete any entry

SIDE EFFECTS
  - Log symptoms (nausea, constipation, headache, fatigue, and more)
    with severity and notes
  - Quick-log chips for fast daily entries

WEIGHT
  - Track starting weight, current weight, and goal weight
  - Full weight history with progress toward your goal

MEASUREMENTS
  - Log body measurements over time (waist, hips, etc.)

CALORIES (protein-first nutrition)
  - Large protein progress ring — your daily protein at a glance
  - Quick-add protein chips: tap a food to log it instantly (26 common foods)
  - Grams / oz toggle (auto-matches your weight unit)
  - Meal type selector (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snack) that defaults to
    the time of day but can be changed before logging
  - Optional manual meal logging (description, calories, protein, fiber)
  - Today's Summary: calories + fiber
  - Daily protein goal auto-calculated from your goal weight (adjustable)

SETTINGS
  - Your Profile: name, age, height
  - Appearance: 5 theme palettes (Rose Calm, Lavender Soft, Sage Wellness,
    Peach Glow, Mocha Neutral)
  - Units & Display: weight, measurement, and hydration units
  - Weight Goals: start/goal weight and dates
  - Daily Nutrition Goals: calories + protein
  - Medication list, protocol phases, and motivational quotes management
  - Injection reminders
  - Data Management: export / import your data as a backup file


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  KEY TOOLS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DOCTOR SUMMARY (the headline feature)
  Generate a clean, printable one-page health report for your appointments.
  Includes a patient header (name, age, height, current weight, BMI), weight
  progress, full injection log, side-effect history, hydration, mood trend,
  and a space for your own questions/notes. Choose a date range, then print
  or save as PDF. Your private journal reflections are NOT included.

FAT BURN ZONE CALCULATOR (in Exercise)
  Enter your age to get your personal fat-burning heart-rate range, so you
  know what to aim for during cardio.

UNIT SYSTEM
  Fully consistent throughout. Choose lbs and protein displays in oz across
  the whole app; choose kg and it displays in grams. Height follows your
  measurement unit (cm, or feet + inches). All values are stored consistently
  behind the scenes, so switching units never corrupts your data.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  DATA & PRIVACY
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - 100% private. All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent
    to any server, and no account is required.

  - IMPORTANT — back up your data. Because everything is stored in your
    browser, clearing your browser data, using private/incognito mode, or
    switching to a different device or browser will erase your history.
    Use Settings -> Data Management -> Export regularly to save a backup file
    you can re-import later.

  - To move to a new device: Export on the old one, open the app on the new
    one, then Import the backup file.
