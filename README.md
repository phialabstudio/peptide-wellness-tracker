# GLP-1 & Peptide Wellness Tracker — User Guide

Your tracker installs to your phone like a normal app — no app store, no account — and it works offline. Here's how to set it up and use it.

---

## Install the app

### iPhone & iPad
Use **Safari** — it won't install from Chrome on iPhone.
1. Open the app link in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** icon (the square with an upward arrow) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.
5. Open it from the new icon — it runs full-screen, no browser bar.

### Android
1. Open the app link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **Install** button on the banner that slides up — or open the **⋮** menu and choose **Install app**.
3. Confirm. The icon lands on your home screen.

### Computer (Chrome or Edge)
Open the link and click the **install icon** in the address bar (or **⋮ → Install**).

---

## Using the tracker

The app opens to seven tabs along the top:

**📊 Dashboard** — your at-a-glance home. See your logging **streak**, recent stats, and quick-log buttons for the day. Start here each day.

**💉 Injection Log** — record each shot: choose the medication, dose, and injection site, then save. The app keeps your history and schedule. Don't see your medication? Add it under **Settings → Add Medication**.

**⚠️ Side Effects** — log any symptom and how severe it is. Over time this reveals patterns you can show your doctor.

**⚖️ Weight** — log your weight and watch the trend move over the weeks.

**📏 Measurements** — record body measurements (waist, etc.) to track changes the scale alone misses.

**🍽️ Calories** — log meals, **protein**, and water. Protein is front and center, since it matters most on GLP-1s.

**⚙️ Settings** — your control center:
- **Medications** — add or edit your medication list.
- **Reminders** — set notifications so you never miss a shot or a log.
- **Backup & Restore** — save a backup file of everything, and load it back later or on a new phone.
- **Doctor Summary** — one tap creates a clean, printable summary of your logs to show or send to your provider.

### Three things worth knowing
- **Reminders** work once the app is **installed**, and on iPhone require **iOS 16.4 or newer**.
- **Backup & Restore** is how you move your history to a new phone — export a backup on the old device, restore it on the new one.
- **Doctor Summary** — export it before appointments, or save one every few weeks as a permanent record.

## Offline & your data

**Works offline.** After you open it online once, it keeps working with no internet — entries save instantly to your device.

**Updates itself.** Reopen the app with internet and it loads the latest version automatically. If something looks off right after an update, fully close and reopen it once.

**Your data stays on your device.** Nothing goes to a server — your logs live privately on your own phone. That keeps it private, but it also means clearing your browser data, uninstalling, or switching phones will **erase your logs unless you've made a backup**. Use **Settings → Backup** regularly, and export a **Doctor Summary** now and then as a second copy.

## Troubleshooting

**The Install button doesn't appear (Android).** Wait a few seconds or revisit the page — your browser offers it once the app is ready to install.

**No Install button on iPhone.** That's normal — iPhone never prompts automatically. Use Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.

**The app looks outdated after an update.** Fully close it and reopen.

**My logs disappeared.** They were stored on this device and got cleared (browser data wiped, app removed, or a new phone). Restore your most recent backup under **Settings → Restore**.
