# GLP-1 & Peptide Wellness Tracker — User Guide

A private, offline-first web app for people on GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound) and peptides. Track injections, protein, weight, body measurements, and side effects — and generate a doctor-ready summary to bring to appointments.

- **Live app:** https://app.phialabstudio.com
- **Product page:** https://phialabstudio.com/glp1-wellness-tracker
- **Maker:** Phia Lab Studio

## What this app does
It is a single-purpose tracker that keeps everything about your GLP-1 / peptide journey in one place and **works fully offline**. Your data stays on your device. The standout feature is a printable **Doctor Summary** so you never blank out at an appointment.

## Install it on your phone or computer (recommended)
The app runs in any browser, but installing it ("Add to Home Screen") gives you a full-screen, app-like experience that opens instantly and works offline.

**iPhone / iPad (use Safari):**
1. Open the app in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an up arrow).
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**. The icon appears on your home screen.

**Android (use Chrome):**
1. Open the app in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu (top right).
3. Tap **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**). An install banner may also appear on its own.
4. Confirm.

**Desktop (Chrome or Edge):**
1. Open the app.
2. Click the **install icon** in the address bar (small monitor/⊕), or **⋮ menu → Install**.

## Using the app — tab by tab
- **Dashboard** — your at-a-glance home: next and last injection, how many you have logged, protein fuel, weight progress, and a daily focus/reflection.
- **Injection Log** — log each shot (medication, dose, date, site). Shows when your next dose is due and your running count.
- **Side Effects** — record symptoms over time. Includes an **Exercise** section with a **Fat Burn Zone Calculator** (heart-rate zones) and a heart-rate field.
- **Protein** — track daily protein against your goal (auto-suggested from your weight goal) with quick-add chips.
- **Weight** — log weight over time; see start → current → change, BMI (needs your height in Settings), and a trend chart.
- **Measurements** — log body measurements (neck, chest, arms, waist, stomach, hips, thighs, calves). The **Body Map** shows your current weight above the figure and your latest measurements in the boxes.
- **Settings** — your profile (name, height, start/goal weight), units, goals, color theme, and backup tools.

## The Doctor Summary (the one to remember)
A clean, printable one-pager that pulls your key numbers together for your provider. Open it from the dashboard or Settings → **🩺 Export Doctor Summary**, then print or save as PDF. Walk in with everything on one page.

## Your data & privacy
- Everything you enter stays **on your device**, in your browser's local storage. It is **not** uploaded to any server.
- That makes it private — but it also means your data is tied to this browser on this device.

## Back up your data (important)
Because your data lives only in your browser, it can be lost if you clear browser data, switch devices, or uninstall.
- **Download a backup:** Settings → **⬇️ Download My Backup** (saves a `.json` file).
- **Restore:** Settings → **⬆️ Restore from Backup** (load that `.json` file).
- Do this regularly, and always before clearing your browser or moving to a new phone.

## Themes
Five color themes in Settings: **Rose Calm** (default), **Lavender Soft**, **Mocha Neutral**, **Peach Glow**, **Sage Wellness**.

## Need help?
If something looks wrong, first try closing and reopening the app. If you recently updated and still see an old version, reinstall it from the website (remove it from your home screen, reopen the site, and add it again).
