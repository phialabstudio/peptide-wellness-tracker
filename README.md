# GLP-1 & Peptide Wellness Tracker

Track your injection schedule, side effects, weight progress, and wellness notes in one calm place.

---

## What This App Does

This is a private, browser-based health tracker designed for people on GLP-1 medications or peptide protocols. Everything you enter is saved on your device only — no accounts, no cloud, no data sharing.

---

## How to Use It

Open `index.html` in any web browser. That's it — no installation needed.

---

## What You Can Track

### Dashboard
Your home screen. See a quick summary of today's check-in: last injection, next scheduled dose, latest weight, hydration, and any side effects logged today.

### Injection Log
Log each injection with the date, time, medication, dose, injection site, and protocol phase. You can filter by medication or phase, and edit or delete any entry.

### Weight
Log your weight over time and see a visual trend chart. Set a goal weight and start date in Settings.

### Calories / Meals
Log meals with an optional description. You don't need to know exact macros — you can pick a protein level (Low / Medium / High / Not sure) and carb level instead, or leave fields blank.

### Measurements
Track body measurements like waist, hips, arms, and more over time.

### Wellness
- **Side Effects** — Select symptoms from a list or type your own. Choose severity (Mild / Moderate / Strong) and add notes.
- **Mood & Energy** — Log how you're feeling each day.
- **Exercise** — Log workouts by type, duration, and intensity. Includes options for Walking, Yoga, Pilates, Strength Training, Zumba, and more.
- **Hydration** — Tap to log water intake throughout the day. Set a daily goal.

### Stats
Visual charts and summaries of your progress over time.

### Settings
- Add or remove medications you want to track
- Set your weight goal and nutrition goals
- Write personal **Medication Instructions** — notes from your provider or pharmacy, your schedule, and questions for your next appointment
- Set browser reminders for injection days
- Change units (lbs / kg, inches / cm)

---

## Your Data

- All data is saved in your browser's local storage
- Nothing is sent to any server
- Use **Download My Backup** in Settings to save a copy of your data as a file
- Use **Restore from Backup** to reload a saved backup
- Use **Download Doctor Summary** (on the Dashboard or in Settings) to generate a printable report you can bring to your doctor

---

## Doctor Summary

The Doctor Summary generates a clean printable report of your health data — injections, weight, side effects, mood, and hydration — for a selected time period (last 7 days, 30 days, or all time). You can add personal notes or questions before printing. Use your browser's print dialog to save it as a PDF.

---

## Medical Disclaimer

This app is for personal organization only and does not provide medical advice, dose calculations, or mixing instructions. Always follow the guidance of your licensed healthcare provider or pharmacy label.

---

## Tips

- You don't have to fill in every field — most are optional
- Use the Medication Instructions section in Settings to keep your provider's notes in one place
- The Doctor Summary is a great way to prepare for check-in appointments
- Download a backup regularly so you don't lose your data if you clear your browser
