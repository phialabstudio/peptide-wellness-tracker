🧬 Peptide Tracker
A beautiful, easy-to-use app to help you track your peptide injections, weight, nutrition, and overall health—all in one place.


________________


📱 What Is This?
Peptide Tracker is like a personal health journal, but much easier than using spreadsheets.


You can track:


* 💉 Peptide injections (which peptide, dose, time, location)
* ⚖️ Weight and progress toward your goal
* 🍽️ Calories and nutrition (protein, carbs, fats, and more)
* 📏 Body measurements (waist, chest, thighs, etc.)
* 😊 Mood, energy, hunger levels
* 🏃 Exercise and workouts
* 😷 Any side effects you notice
* 📝 Meal history and recipes


Everything is saved automatically on your device. Your data is always private—nothing is sent to servers.

🚀 How to Start Using It
Step 1: Open the App
1. You have a file called index.html
2. Double-click it → Opens in your web browser
3. Bookmark it for easy access next time


That's it! The app is ready to use. No login, no waiting.


________________


Step 2: Customize Your Settings (2 minutes)
When you first open the app:


1. Click the ⚙️ Settings tab (bottom right)


2. Choose your units:


   * Pounds (lbs) or Kilograms (kg) for weight
   * Inches (in) or Centimeters (cm) for measurements


3. Set your goals:


   * Enter your starting weight
   * Enter your goal weight
   * Pick your target date (when you want to reach the goal)


4. Choose which peptides you're using:


   * Check the peptides you're tracking (BPC-157, TB-500, etc.)
   * If yours isn't listed, add it under "Peptide List"


5. Choose your extra metric:


   * Pick ONE: Fiber, Sugar, Water Intake, Sodium, or Cholesterol
   * This will appear in your meal logs


6. Add inspirational quotes (optional)


   * Add personal phrases to motivate you ("You can do this!", etc.)

💉 How to Log a Peptide Injection
Click the "📝 Peptide Log" Tab
1. Click "+ Log Peptide" button (light coral/pink)
2. A form appears with these fields:


Fill in:


* Peptide: Choose from dropdown (BPC-157, TB-500, etc.)
* Dose: How much (e.g., 250, 2, 100)
* Date: When did you inject? (calendar picker)
* Time: What time? (e.g., 7:30 AM)
* Site: Where did you inject? (Abdomen L, Abdomen R, Thigh, etc.)
* Phase: Which protocol phase? (Loading, Maintenance, etc.)
* Notes: Optional - "Starting protocol", "Felt good today", etc.
3. Click "Save" → Entry added!


That's it! You'll see a green "Logged successfully" message.

⚖️ How to Track Your Weight
Click the "⚖️ Weight" Tab
You'll see:

* Key numbers:


   * Current weight

To add a new weight:


1. Click "+ Log Weight" button
2. Enter today's weight

The app automatically calculates:


* How much weight you've lost so far

🍽️ How to Track Meals & Calories

   * Recipe link: (optional - paste a URL if you want)


3. Click "Save"


You'll see:


* Remaining calories for the day
* How much of each macro you have left

💾 How to Backup Your Data
Your data is safe on your device, but it's smart to back it up.


To backup:


1. Go to ⚙️ Settings tab
2. Scroll down to "Data Management"
3. Click "Export Data"
4. A file downloads to your computer (named like peptide-tracker-backup.json)
5. Save it somewhere safe (Desktop, cloud drive, etc.)


To restore on a new device:


1. Move the backup file to your new device
2. Open the Peptide Tracker app
3. Go to ⚙️ Settings tab
4. Click "Import Data"
5. Select your backup file
6. All your data appears! ✅

❓ Common Questions
Q: Will my data stay if I close the browser?
✅ Yes! Data is saved automatically and stays forever (until you clear browser data or factory reset).
Q: Can I use this on my phone?
✅ Yes! The app works on phones, tablets, and computers. Just open the file on your phone's browser.
Q: What if I want to use it on multiple devices?
✅ Use the Export/Import feature:


1. Export data on Device A → Get a file
2. Move file to Device B
3. Import on Device B
4. All your data is there!
Q: Can I share this with a friend?
✅ Yes! Share the HTML file. But first:**


1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Delete All Data"
3. Now your friend gets a clean version
4. They can start fresh without seeing your data
Q: The app won't open?
Try:


1. Make sure it's an .html file
2. Double-click it
3. If nothing happens, try:
   * Right-click → "Open with" → Choose your browser
   * Drag the file onto an open browser window
Q: I logged wrong information, how do I fix it?
✅ Click on the entry in the table, edit it, and save!
Q: Can I delete individual entries?
✅ Yes! Find the entry in the table, click the delete button, and confirm.
Q: Is there a mobile app?
❌ No app needed! This works in your phone's web browser. Just open the file and bookmark it.
